Health URLs point to a user's health records portfolio as hosted by a MedCommons licensee. Some hosts may not participate in the global search feature and can be located only by using the full Health URL (e.g.: https://healthurl.myhealthespace.com/1234567890123456 ). Tracking numbers provide access to a limited subset of a Health URL, usually intended to serve as a secure message to the recipient.

An Emergency CCR may be available for some accounts as indicated by the + icon.

Example accounts can be found at 9999-9999-9999-9999 (Password: demo) and 9999-9999-9999 (PIN: 12345).
