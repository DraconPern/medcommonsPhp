Make a MedCommons Widget

A Customized MedCommons Widget can be added to your Google or Yahoo homepage, to your own portal, to the MedCommons IDP portal, or inline in your own wiki, website, or blog. Drag and drop components into your Widget. Cut and paste the generated code into your favorite web applications
